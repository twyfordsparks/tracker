Triangle Tracker.

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices. It is one of the basic shapes in geometry.
We have three types of triangles.

  • Equilateral triangles.

    • -These are triangles with equal sides with equal angles.

  • Isosceles Triangles.

    • -These are triangles with just two equal sides with two equal base angles.

  • Scalene Triangles.

    • These are triangles with no equal sides and non of the angles are equal.
